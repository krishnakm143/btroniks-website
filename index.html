Flagship project

Smart Conveyor Belt Monitoring

A conveyor belt that watches its own health. UHF RFID sensors are embedded inside the belt and scanned continuously as it runs.

Stable signal means the belt is healthy. The instant it tears, the RSSI drops — the system raises a pre-maintenance alarm and asks the operator to continue or stop. One click stops the line, saving the products and preventing further damage.

  • 01Sensors embedded inside the belt, scanned live
  • 02Real-time RSSI & belt-health dashboard
  • 03Automatic pre-maintenance alarm on damage
  • 04Operator decision: continue or stop the line
